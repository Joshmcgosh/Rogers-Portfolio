Pixel Brain

This is my graphic to spread awareness for depression. I wanted to emphasize the brain to remind people that depression is a common disease that many of us suffer from. The wizard was added for levity of a situation that many of us cannot control.

Transforming the Benign

I wanted to show my appreciation for women with this watercolor. This is my rendering of an everyday woman that belongs to a small town and her culture is literally ingrained around her mind. She is content with her home but daydreams about leaving.

"The Best We Could Do" 'By Thi Bui'

This was a book we read in my Graphic Design class. In class, we tried to recreate the spirit of the original book cover, but I wanted to show the setting of the story by conveying the Bay Bridge skyline which I hope to one day see.
